About Us

Welcome to Mpingana Secondary School

Mpingana is amidst the rural landscape of the Eastarn Cape Province, is a public secondary school offering ordinary education. Located at Ngquza A/A Sixhontweni, Flagstaff 4810 in the vibrant suburb of Lusikisiki, Holly Cross South Africa. It stands as a beacon of dedicated learning within the Eastern Cape community. Currently, we offer only 2 streams (Science in class A and Accounting in class B).

  • Provide intellectual growth and development for its learners
  • Has dedicaded teachers guiding and mentoring students
  • Operates under the education District, Promoting educational excellence
  • Aims to create conductive learning environment for all students

    • Mpingana follows the outh African public school structure. This means the are no fees or monthly payments required.
